From Buzz to Business Need

The opening turns broad market momentum into a concrete systems problem: how to handle modern customer support with AI.

Study

The deck starts by shrinking the problem on purpose

The presentation begins with agentic AI as a mainstream pattern across major vendors, but it does not stay at the level of product buzz. It quickly narrows to customer support in 2024, when ChatGPT was already familiar and APIs were mature enough to build real systems. That move matters because the rest of the deck is not about AI in general; it is about choosing an architecture for a practical workflow problem.

  • The speaker separates workflow agents from autonomous agents immediately.
  • This session is explicitly about workflow agents, not the autonomous branch.
  • The motivating use case is customer support, where requests can vary by intent and tone.
  • The opening is doing architectural setup, not offering a vendor comparison.
